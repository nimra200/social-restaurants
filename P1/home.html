What is Restify?


Restify is a restaurants one stop shop for showcasing all it has to offer. A
gathering place for restaurants of all kinds and types. Through Restify you
can get in touch and learn more about restaurants near you and what
they have to offer and maybe where your next meal can be!

What can I do on Restify?


Restify allows you to experience a fully integrated social network platform with the ability to do things like post stories, follow and see the latest news and posts from your favorite restaurants and be notified about the activity of other users and resturants on the site. Restify also allows you to search for info on resturants and people on the network with the search bar above!

How do I start?


Joining the Restify experience is simple! Simply sign up here and then you can begin viewing and following all of your favoirte restaurants and the people who work to make them what they are! If you already have an account then you can login in here and start browsing.
